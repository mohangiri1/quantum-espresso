Two-Week Quantum ESPRESSO Learning Plan

Week 1: Fundamentals and Basic Operations

  • Day 1:

    Introduction to Quantum ESPRESSO

    • Overview of Quantum ESPRESSO capabilities and applications
    • Installing Quantum ESPRESSO on your system
    • Explore the Quantum ESPRESSO website and documentation
  • Day 2:

    Basic Quantum Mechanics and DFT Concepts

    • Review of key quantum mechanics principles
    • Introduction to Density Functional Theory (DFT)
    • Understanding pseudopotentials and plane-wave basis sets
  • Day 3:

    Getting Started with Quantum ESPRESSO

    • Running your first calculation: SCF (Self-Consistent Field) calculation
    • Understanding the input and output files
    • Using the PWscf module
  • Day 4:

    Structure Optimization

    • Running a basic structure optimization
    • Convergence tests: energy cutoff and k-points
    • Analyzing optimization results
  • Day 5:

    Electronic Structure Calculations

    • Performing non-SCF calculations
    • Density of States (DOS) and Band Structure calculations
    • Plotting DOS and band structures
  • Day 6:

    Advanced Topics and Troubleshooting

    • Dealing with convergence issues
    • Advanced input options
    • Common errors and how to fix them
  • Day 7:

    Review and Practice

    • Review the week's material
    • Work on example problems
    • Join Quantum ESPRESSO forums and discuss with peers

Week 2: Advanced Usage and Applications

  • Day 8:

    Phonon Calculations

    • Introduction to phonon calculations
    • Running a basic phonon calculation
    • Analyzing phonon dispersion results
  • Day 9:

    Mechanical Properties

    • Calculating elastic constants
    • Studying material deformation
    • Analyzing stress-strain relationships
  • Day 10:

    Magnetic Properties

    • Introduction to spin-polarized calculations
    • Performing magnetic moment calculations
    • Analyzing spin density results
  • Day 11:

    Surface and Interface Calculations

    • Modeling surfaces and interfaces
    • Running slab calculations
    • Analyzing surface energy and work function
  • Day 12:

    Using Advanced Modules and Plugins

    • Introduction to CP (Car-Parrinello) and TDDFT (Time-Dependent DFT)
    • Exploring other Quantum ESPRESSO modules
    • Using external tools and plugins for visualization
  • Day 13:

    Project Work

    • Select a small project based on your research interests
    • Set up and run simulations
    • Analyze and interpret the results
  • Day 14:

    Final Review and Future Steps

    • Review the advanced topics covered
    • Identify areas needing further study
    • Plan next steps for in-depth learning and research
